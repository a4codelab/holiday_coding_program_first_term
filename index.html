Yes, a laptop or desktop computer is required. Unfortunately, tablets and phones won't work well for coding. The device should have a stable internet connection and a modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, or Edge).

A minimum of 2 Mbps is recommended for smooth video streaming. However, 5 Mbps or higher is ideal for the best experience. Students should test their connection before the first class.

Does my child need prior coding experience?

No prior experience is needed! This program is designed for complete beginners. We'll start from the very basics and build up gradually. All your child needs is curiosity and eagerness to learn.

What age group is this program for?

This program is ideal for secondary school students (ages 10-18). The content is tailored to be engaging and understandable for this age range, with projects that relate to Nigerian culture and daily life.

After registration, you'll receive an email with detailed payment instructions, including bank account details and payment confirmation steps. Once payment is confirmed, you'll receive the class link and schedule.
